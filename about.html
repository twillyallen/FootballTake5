Pigskin 5

- Pigskin5 is a daily NFL trivia game: 5 questions, 15 seconds each.
- Questions mix NFL history, current season storylines and statistics, lovable players, and random facts!
- Keep your Daily Streak alive and score a Touchdown (perfect 5/5).

Built for fun as a passion project by Twillyallen.
